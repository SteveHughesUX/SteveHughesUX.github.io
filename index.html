About Me

With over 8 years of experience in User Experience Design, I specialize in creating meaningful and intuitive digital products. My journey in UX began with a fascination for human behavior and a desire to bridge the gap between technology and usability. I thrive on collaborating with cross-functional teams, from product managers to engineers, to bring innovative ideas to life.

I believe that great design is not just about aesthetics, but about deep understanding of user needs, rigorous testing, and iterative refinement. My expertise spans across user research, wireframing, prototyping, usability testing, and design system implementation. When I'm not designing, you can find me hiking in nature or exploring new coffee shops.
